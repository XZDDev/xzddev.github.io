ReleaseQuest

Never miss the next Nintendo release. Discover upcoming and recently released games, build personal game lists, and keep track of confirmed release dates for Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch 2.

iOS Android macOS

Overview

ReleaseQuest helps Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch 2 players discover upcoming and recently released games, build personal game lists, and keep track of confirmed release dates.

Choose the consoles and genres you care about, then browse a personalised feed of upcoming releases or explore games launched during the last 30 days. View game artwork, release information, genres, countdowns, descriptions, trailers, Metacritic scores, platform and edition details, and more, all in one place.

Features

  • Follow Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, or both.
  • Discover upcoming releases based on your favourite genres.
  • Browse games released during the last 30 days.
  • Search for games and view detailed release information.
  • See platform, edition, publisher, age rating, player count, download size, and format details when available.
  • Organise games into Wishlist, Playing, Ongoing, Backlog, or Completed.
  • Search My Lists and sort games by release date or title.
  • Add private personal notes to games in My Lists.
  • Get optional alerts when a wishlisted game's release date changes.
  • See confirmed Wishlist release dates in your personal calendar.
  • Add confirmed game launches to your device's system calendar.
  • Filter releases by console and genre.
  • Use the release calendar home screen widget so you never miss an important launch.
  • View Metacritic scores for the games you care about.
  • Back up and move your game lists and personal notes between devices.

No account or subscription required.

ReleaseQuest is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Nintendo.
