Paizo

Paizo is a modern PlayStation companion app for iOS, iPadOS, macOS and Apple Watch. Built to keep your gaming life organized. Track your library, explore game details, view trophies, follow friends, and customize the experience.

Download on the App Store

Features

  • Search for PlayStation games with rich details, artwork, screenshots, trailers, and release dates.
  • Platinum Showcase, showcase your top 10 proudest Platinum trophies.
  • Weekly Summary, see everything you and all your friends have been up to the past 7 days.
  • Paizo Apple Watch Companion application, see friends and your games from your wrist.
  • HomeScreen & LockScreen Widgets to show your Wishlist calendar, most recent trophy and recently played game.
  • Build and organize your personal game library with statuses like Playing, Still to Play, Wishlist, and Completed.
  • View your PlayStation profile, recent activity, trophy summary, and earned trophy collection.
  • Browse friends, recent games, and trophy activity.
  • Open trophy guides using your preferred provider, including PowerPyx, PSNProfiles, PlayStation Trophies and TrueTrophies.
  • Customize the app with themes, icon colors, and appearance settings.

Paizo is designed for PlayStation players who want a clean, stylish way to manage their games and trophies in one place.
